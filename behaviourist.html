Dr. Emily Hernandez

Dr. Emily Hernandez is a renowned figure in veterinary medicine, specializing in cardiology for animals. With over two decades of experience and a distinguished career, Dr. Hernandez has dedicated her expertise to diagnosing and treating heart conditions in animals. Her profound understanding of cardiovascular diseases in pets has led to innovative treatments and life-saving interventions. Her commitment to providing comprehensive care and advanced solutions has earned her recognition as a pioneer in veterinary cardiology, making her an invaluable resource for pet owners seeking specialized heart care for their beloved animals.

Dr. Thomas Bennett

Dr. Thomas Bennett stands as a leading veterinary neurologist known for his exceptional work in diagnosing and treating neurological disorders in animals. With a strong foundation in veterinary neurology and neurosurgery, Dr. Bennett addresses a wide range of neurological conditions in pets, including seizures, spinal disorders, and nerve injuries. His expertise lies in employing cutting-edge diagnostic techniques and developing tailored treatment plans to improve the quality of life for animals with neurological issues. Dr. Bennett's dedication to advancing the field of veterinary neurology has positioned him as a key figure in providing specialized care for pets facing complex neurological challenges.

Her commitment to resolving skin-related problems in animals through precise diagnosis and individualized treatment strategies has earned her accolades in the veterinary community. Dr. Lee's specialized knowledge and compassionate approach make her a sought-after professional for pet owners seeking expert care for their furry companions' skin health.

More about TRAINING

Kuddle's dog training service in Bengaluru specializes in tackling challenges faced by pet parents, such as addressing concerns like how to stop puppy biting, and guiding them effectively through successful toilet training for developing good toilet habits. Our experienced and certified dog trainers undergo a comprehensive onboarding procedure as part of our canine dog training program. They skillfully address intricate issues by curbing dog biting tendencies, implementing effective dog potty training techniques, mitigating dog separation anxiety, tackling dog aggression issues, and promoting pet socialization.

Our tailored programs encompass a wide range of canine education, covering aspects from puppy training to dog potty training, ensuring that you can train your dog effectively. We cater to various breeds and ages, such as Shih Tzu, German Shepherds, Golden Retrievers, Rottweilers, Labradors, and Dobermans. Our approach involves equipping pet parents to actively engage in the training journey, fostering a strong bond between dogs and their Hoomans.
