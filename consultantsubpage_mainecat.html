Persian Cat Common Health Concerns

Persian Cats are known for their distinctive appearance, but they may be prone to certain health concerns. Let's explore some common issues.

Respiratory Issues

Persian Cats may experience respiratory issues due to their flat faces and brachycephalic features.

Dental Problems

Dental problems, including gingivitis and periodontal disease, are common in Persian Cats. Regular dental care is essential.

Obesity

Persian Cats are prone to obesity. Monitor their diet and encourage play to maintain a healthy weight.

Eye Conditions

Eye conditions such as tear staining and eye infections can occur in Persian Cats. Regular eye cleaning is recommended.
